Lasagna

Description

A classic lasagna is a baked Italian casserole consisting of layered pasta sheets, savory meat-tomato sauce (ragù), and a cheese mixture, typically finished with melted mozzarella.

Ingredients

  • ground beef/pork
  • onion
  • garlic
  • tomatoes
  • lasagna noodles

Steps

  1. Prepare the Sauce: Brown ground beef and pork sausage with onion and garlic, then stir in tomato paste, tomatoes, and herbs; simmer for at least 20 minutes.
  2. Prepare the Noodles: Boil lasagna noodles until al dente, or use oven-ready sheets to save time.
  3. Make the Cheese Mixture: Mix ricotta cheese with parmesan, mozzarella, and an egg (to hold the shape).
  4. Assemble the Layers:Base: Spread a thin layer of meat sauce in the dish to prevent sticking.Layer 1: Noodles, cheese mixture, meat sauce, and mozzarella.Repeat: Repeat layering 3-4 times, finishing with sauce and mozzarella on top.
  5. Bake: Cover with foil and bake at \(375^{\circ }\text{F}\) (\(190^{\circ }\text{C}\)) for 30 minutes, then uncover and bake for another 15-20 minutes until bubbling.
  6. Rest: Let the lasagna sit for 10-15 minutes before cutting to allow layers to set.
