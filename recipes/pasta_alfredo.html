Pasta_alfredo

Description

Fettuccine Alfredo is a rich, creamy Italian-American pasta dish made by tossing hot pasta (traditionally fettuccine) in a sauce of melted butter and Parmesan cheese

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Butter
  • Cheese
  • Heavy cream
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions.
  2. Melt the butter in a large skillet over medium high heat. Add the garlic and cook for 30 seconds, or until fragrant.
  3. Pour in the milk and cream.
  4. Remove the pan from the heat and mix in the cooked pasta until the sauce begins to thicken
